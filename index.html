About Kevin

A journalist who speaks your language — and theirs

Kevin Feddy is a vastly experienced and respected journalist who spent more than 25 years working on daily newspapers. He was Business Editor at the Manchester Evening News for 12 years — one of the UK's most-read regional titles.

That newsroom background isn't a badge on a wall. It's a practical edge: Kevin knows precisely what editors look for, how stories get spiked, and how to frame your news so it actually gets picked up. He writes like a journalist. Because he is one.

Since launching his own agency he has worked with businesses of all sizes — from growing SMEs to established organisations — delivering communications that are personal, proactive, and built around your goals.
