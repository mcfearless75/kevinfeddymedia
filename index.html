About Kevin

A journalist who speaks your language — and theirs

Kevin Feddy is a vastly experienced and respected journalist who spent more than 25 years working on daily newspapers, including 12 years as Business Editor at the Manchester Evening News.

That newsroom background isn't a badge — it's a practical advantage. Kevin knows exactly what editors look for, how to frame a story for maximum pickup, and how to write copy that connects with real audiences rather than sounding like marketing.

Since launching his own agency, Kevin has worked with businesses across sectors — from SMEs to established organisations — providing communications services that are proactive, personal, and built around your goals.
